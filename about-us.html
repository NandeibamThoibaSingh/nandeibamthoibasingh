About Our Institute

Welcome to NTS Institute of Technology

NTS Insitute of Technology was established in 2017. This Institute has been established with a mission to provide quality education to students in the field of IT sector to enable them to serve the society in all possible ways. We believe in creating and spreading knowledge in different areas through Innovative Educational Programs and Research along with a High Level of Skill and Management. We, NTS Insitute of Technology, have created a very friendly learning environment with the latest Computer Hardware and Software Technologies. Whether you are new to the computer industry or are looking to improve your skills, we have the Right Course for you. Our class features includes:

  • Well trained faculties
  • Free study materials
  • 1 PC per student
  • Offline/Online Class
  • Smart Class
